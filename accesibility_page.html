Accesibility
The main menu
The main menu is created in a way that makes every page easy to access it.Each button, directs you to the page with the same name.
The home page
The home page has a short description of myself containing information about my hometown, what I've done in the last 3 years and what I am doing now as a student at University of Sheffield at Computer Science.
The degree page
The degree page contains information about all the modules that I am studing this year as a student at Computer Science. Each module, has a figure related to its content, the time when I have to attend to the online lectures and a brief explanation of what students learn after they attend to the course. At the end of the page, we have a link to the site of University of Sheffield where we can acces it by cliking the word "here". Also, we have an suggest to go to the quiz page by clicking "quiz" and to see the data accesing the "data" page. .
The quiz page
The quiz page can be accesed only from the degree page.The quiz page has 3 questions. Each question is related to the pictures. What we have to do, is to find the perfect word that matches in the "..." or to answer to a question. We can see the right answer by hovering over "show the correct answer" and the answer will be shown at the bottom of the display on a purple background with white font color.
The data visualisation page
It can be accesed only from degree page. The data visualisation page, shows in a table the data that we will use, calculated in hours. Then it is displayed an graphic that can be changed by cliking the "Change" button.
The contact page
The contact page contains my name , my student email and the adress of the departament of Computer Science. Then the page offers us the oportunity to send a comment by email.
